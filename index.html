What is R2M

Many organizations help you explore opportunities. R2M helps you execute.

R2M acts as an Export Management Company (EMC) and international business development partner. We help Canadian companies move from interest to execution through a structured export pathway that reduces risk and accelerates traction.

We also help global buyers access trusted Canadian suppliers through a professional, transparent process.
